Ricky Bulda is an actor based out of San Diego and the Los Angeles Area. With over 200 productions over his career, he is well versed on stage in musical theater, ballet, and opera. Starting his career at the Starlight Bowl in San Diego, he has performed all over the western region of the United States. On TV he has appeared on Disney's ENCORE with Kristen Bell, TERRIERS on F/X, and General Hospital. He also has credits as an assistant/associate choreographer and director, and dance captain of numerous productions.
